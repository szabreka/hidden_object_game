How to Play the Hidden Object Game

Explore the world of art and solve riddles to uncover hidden objects within famous paintings from the Rijksmuseum. Follow these simple rules to enjoy the game:

  1. Solve the Riddle: Each object is represented by a unique riddle. Solve the riddle to unveil the hidden object.
  2. Search the Painting: Examine the painting to find the hidden object. Be observant, as there might be multiple occurrences, but we're looking for just one of them.
  3. Click to Reveal: Once you spot the hidden object, click on it to reveal its details.
  4. Discover the Artwork: Learn more about the artwork displayed in the Rijksmuseum. Each painting has a story to tell, and every hidden object is a piece of that narrative.

Challenge yourself, enjoy the beauty of art, and have fun uncovering the secrets within the frames!
